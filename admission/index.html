Fee Refund Policy in Case of Withdrawal/Cancellation of Admission:

  1. On or before the date of Financial Registration: Full fees will be refunded after deducting the processing charges of Rs. 2,500/-
  2. After the date of Financial Registration: No fees will be refunded. In case the candidate pays full fees, only the caution deposit will be refunded and mess charges will be collected on a pro rata basis.
