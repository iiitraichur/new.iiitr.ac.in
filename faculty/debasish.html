Introduction

Currently, I am working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of CSE, IIIT Raichur. I joined IIIT Raichur on 1 st June 2022. Prior to this, I was working as an Assistant Professor in Christ (Deemed to be University), Bangalore for the past 2 years and 10 months. I have completed PhD from IIT(ISM) Dhanbad under the supervision of Prof. Susanta Mukhopadhyay. I have also served as a reviewer of reputed journals like IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems – I: Regular Papers, ETRI Journal – Wiley.
