Introduction

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics, Indian Institute of Information Technology, Raichur. I have completed my Doctoral degree from Jadavpur University in 2015. I have worked as a Post-doctoral Fellow in Indian Institute of Science, and also as a Personal Investigator in the Department of Mathematics, Universidad de Granada, Spain.

Research Interests

  • Geometry of Banach spaces
  • Birkhoff-James orthogonality
  • Optimization in Banach spaces
  • Topological Vector Spaces
