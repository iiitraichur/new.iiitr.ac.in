Introduction

I am an Assistant Professor in Computer Science and Engineering at IIIT Raichur. I joined the institute in November 2022. Prior to joining IIIT Raichur, I was an Assistant Professor at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Dehradun. Before working at UPES, I was a postdoctoral research fellow at Complex Networks Dynamics Lab, Bar-Ilan University, Israel. I did my Ph.D. degree in Network Science from IIT Indore. My research centers on network science especially focusing on understanding the information propagation pattern in complex networks arising from nonlinear interactions. Besides, I am exploring time series data analysis in the domain of network science and machine learning-based data-driven modeling of complex systems.

Research Interests

  • Network Science
  • Nonlinear Dynamics
  • Machine Learning
  • Time Series Data Analysis
  • Quantum Information
