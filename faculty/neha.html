Introduction

I am an Assistant Professor in Computer Science and Engineering at IIIT Raichur. I joined the institute in January 2023. Prior to joining IIIT Raichur, I was an Assistant Professor at the Chandigarh University, Punjab. I completed a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from NIT Jalandhar under the supervision of Dr Geeta Sikka and Prof. Lalit Kumar Awasthi in 2021.
