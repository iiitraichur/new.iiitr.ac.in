IIIT Raichur is one of the new IIITs proposed by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India, located at Raichur, Karnataka state. IIIT Raichur has started its academic activity in August 2019 with its first batch of Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering. As the mentor institute, IIT Hyderabad is facilitating the functioning of IIIT Raichur in the IIT Hyderabad campus. The students are hosted in IIT Hyderabad campus and will attend classes at the Institute.

IIIT Raichur will be functioning from its transit campus at IIT Hyderabad until the infrastructure at the temporary/permanent campus at Raichur is ready.
