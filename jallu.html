Introduction

Currently, I am working as an Assistant Professor at IIIT Raichur. I joined the institute on 8th January, 2021. I am happy to be the first faculty of IIIT Raichur. Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Institute of Computer Science of The Czech Academy of Sciences, Czech Republic. I was a team member of the project "Structural properties of visibility in terrains and farthest color Voronoi diagrams", funded by the Czech Science Foundation. The PI of the project is Dr. Maria Saumell. I love to work and design (approximation) algorithms for the problems that have inherent geometry.
