DIRECTIONS TO REACH IIIT RAICHUR CAMPUS @ IIT HYDERABD

All the activities of IIIT Raichur are running in new campus of IIT Hyderabad. All classes and academic activities are being conducted in IITH Kandi Campus. Accomodation is also provided in IIT Hyderabad Campus.

Kandi Campus: It is located on the National Highway 9 at Kandi village near Sangareddy town in Telangana.


To reach IIT Hyderabad Campus

From RGI Airport:

Kandi campus is located at a distance of about 65 kms from the airport. It is possible to hire taxi (between Rs. 1200/- to Rs. 1400/-, depending on the vehicle used) to reach the campus. It takes about 1 hr 20 min drive from RGI Airport to IIT Kandi campus (via Jawaharlal Nehru Outer Ring rd).

Airport – IITH: 65 KMS (1 hr 20mins)

1. Airport – outer ring road towards Gachibowli – BHEL – Patancheru – Isnapur – IITH(Kandi)

2. Airport – Outer Ring Road Exit Patancheru – Isnapur – IITH .

Bus route: Reach Mehdipatnam (27 km from Airport ) by bus then board 217 or 222 towards Patancheru (27kms from Mehdipatnam). Then board bus towards Sangareddy. IIT Hyderabad is located 5km ahead of Sangareddy.

RGI Airport - Mehdipatnam - Patancheru - Isnapur cross road - IIT Hyderabad - Sangareddy.

From Mahatma Gandhi Bus station:

IIT Hyderabad is located around 53 kms from MGBS. Buses (express) are available directly to Sangareddy from MGBS. Buses going towards Zaheerabad, Bidar also reach Sangareddy. IIT Hyderabad is located 5km ahead of Sangareddy. These buses have stops only at Patancheru and Sangareddy. There is a request bus stop at IIT Hyderabad. You can also reach Patancheru from CBS bus stop (located just outside MGBS) by boarding the bus 218C.

MGBS - (approx 35kms) Patancheru - (approx 18kms)- IIT Hyderabad - Sangareddy.

From Secunderabad Railway Station:

Secunderabad Station is located about 52 kms from campus. You can hire pre-paid taxis at station. The typical cost will be between Rs. 1200/- to 1400/-

Bus route: Board the bus 219 or 226 towards Patancheru then board the bus towards Sangareddy.

Secunderabad – IITH : 52 KMS (Time : 1.30 hrs.)

Secunderabad – Bowenpally – Balanagar – Kukatpally – Miyapur –Lingampally (BHEL) - Patancheru – Isnapur – IITH.

From Kacheguda Railway Station:

Kacheguda station is located about 53 kms from IIT Hyderabad. You can hire a taxi which will cost Rs 700/- to 800/-.

Bus route: There are no direct buses from Kacheguda to Patancheru. Reach Koti (5kms from Kacheguda) by 3SS, 3z, 352. Then board the bus 218, 218C, 217D, 225D 222 or 222A to reach Patancheru. Then board the bus towards Sangareddy. IIT Hyderabad is located 5km ahead of Sangareddy. There is are quest stop at IIT Hyderabad.

Kachiguda (6kms )- Koti –(approx. 35 km )- Patancheru - Muthangi - Isnapur cross road - IIT Hyderabad - Sangareddy.

From Nampally Railway Station:

Nampally station is located about 50 kms from IIT Hyderabad. You can hire a taxi which will cost Rs 700/- to 800/-.

Bus route: Board the bus 218 or 225 towards Patancheru. Then board the bus towards Sangareddy. IIT Hyderabad is located 5km ahead of Sangareddy. There is a request stop at IIT Hyderabad.

Nampally - (approx. 32 km ) Patancheru - Muthangi \rightarrow Isnapur cross road - IIT Hyderabad - Sangareddy.

Other Routes:

Gachibowli – IIT Hyderabad

Via Lingampally – Patancheru – Isnapur – IITH. (Time 1hr 30 minutes, 25 kms.)

Mehdipatnam – IITH : 45 KMS (Time : 1.15 hrs)

Mehdipatnam – Haath Statue – Gachibowli – BHEL – Patancheru – Isnapur - IITH.

Miyapur – IITH : 30 KMS (45 minutes)

Miyapur – Lingampally (BHEL) – Patancheru – Isnapur – IITH.
