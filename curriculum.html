Curriculum

The IIIT Raichur BTech program will use the innovative fractal academics, pioneered by IIT Hyderabad. This allows the students benefit from the T-education model, and gain both breadth of knowledge and depth on the subjects of their interest.

The B.Tech. program consists of eight semesters spread over four years. An Academic Year (July-April) consists of 2 semesters, each of approximately 16 weeks duration. The first semester begins in the last week of July / first week of August and ends by the last week of November. The second semester starts in the first week of January and ends by the first week of May. A student is required to register the relevant courses listed in the curriculum for that semester.

Facilities

Students will be provided all the necessary infrastructure facilities, like library, computing, networking, workshops, teaching laboratories and research laboratories. IIIT Raichur temporary campus at IIT Hyderabad will be a residential campus. All hostels will be equipped with sport, recreation rooms, TV room, washing machines, and dining etc. Ho Shstels will be equipped with Wi-Fi access. IIT Hyderabad permanent campus at Kandi has a good sports ground. Students can get involved in numerous sports to enable them maintain their physical fitness and develop team spirit.

Students of IIIT Raichur will be a part of all the student activities taking place at IITH. IIT Hyderabad is a residential campus, with well facilitated students’ hostels. All hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, solar water heaters and solar lighting. Recreation rooms, Table Tennis rooms, Dance room, Music Room, Robotics room and TV room etc. give students a platform to go beyond the Academics. IITH has sports grounds both in Hostel campus and institute. Students are involved in numerous sports to enable them maintain their physical fitness and develop team spirit. IITH provides hygienic mess facilities for all students with menu changed, if necessary on a monthly basis. There is a bus service connecting the institute building with the hostels with good frequency.
