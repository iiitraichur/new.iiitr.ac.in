The IIIT Raichur BTech program will use the innovative fractal academics, pioneered by IIT Hyderabad. This allows the students benefit from the T-education model, and gain both breadth of knowledge and depth on the subjects of their interest.

The B.Tech. program consists of eight semesters spread over four years. An Academic Year (July-April) consists of 2 semesters, each of approximately 16 weeks duration. The odd semester begins in the last week of July / first week of August and ends by the last week of November. The even semester starts in the first week of January and ends by the first week of May. A student is required to register the relevant courses listed in the curriculum for that semester.
