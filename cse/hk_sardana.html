Introduction

Dr. Harish K Sardana, currently Director IIIT Raichur since 1 Dec 2022 was previously a Member, Arunachal Pradesh Pvt Edu Inst Regulatory Commission (APPEIRC), Itanagar since May 2022. Prior to that he was Chief Scientist at CSIR-Central Scientific Instruments Organisation, Chandigarh, India till Nov 2021. He had joined CSIO in December 1982.

Research Interests

His research interests include image processing, signal/image processing, pattern recognition, and machine intelligence in Medical/Strategic/Safety/Security/Agro sectors.

Education

He started his career in 1982 after completing his graduation in Electronics and Communication Engineering from REC (now NIT) Kurukshetra. During his career, he accomplished M.E. from PEC Chandigarh and Ph.D. from the University of Nottingham, the UK in 1989 and 1993 respectively. He completed his Ph.D. in the UK under Nehru Centenary British Fellowship (FCO) scholarship during 1990-93 in the area of image processing and pattern recognition. In 2001, he completed his MBA in Human Resource Management and Organisation Behaviour. He obtained merit in CSIR Leadership Development Programme for middle to senior scientists in 2007 and has been a faculty for the same.

Experience

He has 23 Patents granted and 6 pending to his credit. He has more than 24 PGs and 18 PhDs completed and a few are under process. He has delivered about 150 invited presentations in national and international forums, many of them are available on YouTube. He has been the IEEE-Student Branch Counsellor (in AcSIR-CSIO, Chandigarh) during 2019-21.

Sponsored Projects

Dr Sardana completed more than 20 sponsored projects worth more than 70 Cr during the last two decades. In addition, he was consultant to two industry projects. More than 15 projects were completed by him as Mentor/Co-PI in other projects.
