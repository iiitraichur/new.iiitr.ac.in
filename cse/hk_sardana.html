Introduction

Dr. Harish Kumar Sardana, currently Director IIIT Raichur since 1 December 2022 was previously a Member, of Arunachal Pradesh Pvt Edu Inst Regulatory Commission (APPEIRC), Itanagar since May 2022. Before that he was Chief Scientist at CSIR-Central Scientific Instruments Organisation, Chandigarh, India till Nov 2021. He joined CSIO in December 1982.
He has been a Coordinator (2009-17) and Professor at the Academy of Scientific & Innovative Research - AcSIR-CSIO, Chandigarh. He continued to have a dual designation of Professor also at AcSIR-CSIO Chandigarh till his superannuation in November 2021

Research Interests

His research interests include image processing, signal/image processing, pattern recognition, and machine intelligence in Medical/Strategic/Safety/Security/Agro sectors.

Education

He started his career in 1982 after completing his graduation in Electronics and Communication Engineering from REC (now NIT) Kurukshetra. During his career, he accomplished M.E. from PEC Chandigarh and a Ph.D. from the University of Nottingham, the UK in 1989 and 1993 respectively. He completed his Ph.D. in the UK under Nehru Centenary British Fellowship (FCO) scholarship during 1990-93 in the area of image processing and pattern recognition. In 2001, he completed his MBA in Human Resource Management and Organisation Behaviour. He obtained merit in CSIR Leadership Development Programme for middle to senior scientists in 2007 and has been a faculty for the same.
